About V Rising Guides

1,200+ hours. 64 bosses. Every guide backed by real gameplay — not wiki rewrites.

🧛

Alex — V Rising Master Player

Logged 1,200+ hours in Vardoran. Every V Blood boss killed on Brutal difficulty across multiple playthroughs. This site exists because most V Rising guides stop at boss #20 — I wanted one that covers all 64.

Why I Built This Site

When the Invaders of Oakveil expansion dropped, I went looking for an updated boss kill order. Every guide I found covered the first 20-30 bosses, then stopped. The wiki had the data but no strategy — just names, numbers, and locations. I wanted something that combined both: complete data and practical strategy grounded in actual play experience.

So I built it myself. Every boss order, every weapon tier, every blood type recommendation on this site comes from personal testing. When this guide says "Kill Vincent before Leandra because Frost Bat Form lets you skip ground mobs in Silverlight," it's because I've done that route five times and timed the difference.

How Guides Are Tested

I don't publish anything I haven't verified in-game:

  • Boss strategies — Tested on Brutal difficulty. If a strategy works on Brutal, it works on Normal.
  • Weapon rankings — Each weapon tested against 3 boss types at equal gear score. DPS measured, not guessed.
  • Blood type data — Quality thresholds retested after every major patch. Stunlock tweaks numbers; I recheck.
  • Resource farming routes — Timed. I track how much ore you get per minute at each farming spot.

Corrections Policy

V Rising gets patched. Guides go stale. If you find an error or outdated info, email me at alex@vrisingguides.com. I fix verified errors within 24 hours and credit the reporter (if you want).

Not a Wiki, Not AI-Generated

This site is hand-written by one person who plays too much V Rising. No AI rewrites wiki data and calls it a guide. No faceless content farm. Just a player who wanted complete, tested guides — and figured other players might want them too.
