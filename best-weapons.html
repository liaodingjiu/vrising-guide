S-TIER — Best Weapons in V Rising

These three weapons dominate the meta. If you can only craft one weapon per tier, start here.

🪦 Reaper — S-Tier PvE / A-Tier PvP

The Reaper is the undisputed king of PvE mob clearing. Its wide swing arcs and area-of-effect abilities let you tear through packs of enemies faster than any other weapon.

Key Abilities:
  • Tendon Swing (Q): Wide cleave that snares enemies — excellent for controlling groups while dealing heavy damage.
  • Howl (E): AoE fear that creates breathing room when surrounded.
  • Bonestorm (Ultimate): Devastating channeled AoE that shreds everything in range.
Pro Tip: The Reaper's snare from Tendon Swing makes it viable in PvP for coordinated team fights, but its slow attack speed hurts in 1v1 duels against mobile enemies.

🏹 Longbow — S-Tier PvE / S-Tier PvP

The safest weapon in V Rising. The Longbow lets you deal maximum damage from maximum distance — invaluable against tough bosses and deadly in PvP.

Key Abilities:
  • Focus (Passive/Stacks): Each consecutive hit increases your damage. Maintain stacks during boss fights for significant DPS increases.
  • Rain of Arrows (Q): Large AoE carpet bomb — excellent for clearing adds during boss fights.
  • Snap Shot (E): Quick burst damage that slows enemies, creating kiting distance.
Pro Tip: Focus stacks are the Longbow's hidden power. A full-stack Longbow out-damages every other weapon on single targets. Never let your stacks drop during a boss fight.

🔱 Spear — S-Tier PvE / S-Tier PvP

The Spear offers unmatched utility with its Harpoon ability, which pulls enemies toward you. In PvE it repositions bosses; in PvP it guarantees kills.

Key Abilities:
  • Harpoon (Q): Pulls an enemy to you, interrupting their attack. Pulls bosses out of danger zones. Pulls fleeing PvP opponents back into combat.
  • A Thousand Spears (E): Multiple fast stabs in a cone — among the safest and highest DPS skills in the game.
  • Shackle (Ultimate): Roots enemies in place while dealing damage over time.
Pro Tip: Harpoon is the best repositioning tool in V Rising. Use it to pull ranged bosses into melee range, yank V Blood bosses out of AoE puddles, or pull enemy players off high ground.

A-TIER — Strong, Situationally Excellent

These weapons excel in specific contexts. Build around them and they rival S-tier.

🗡️ Slasher — B-Tier PvE / S-Tier PvP

The Slasher is the PvP weapon. Its Camouflage ability makes you invisible — the single most powerful tool in player-versus-player combat.

Key Abilities:
  • Camouflage (Q): Turn invisible for several seconds. Break stealth with an attack for bonus backstab damage. The element of surprise wins PvP fights.
  • Blinding Fury (E): Multi-hit dash that deals damage and disorients opponents.
Pro Tip: In PvP, Camouflage lets you escape certain death, reposition for backstabs, or simply wait out enemy cooldowns. It's a get out of jail free card on a short cooldown.

🔫 Pistols — A-Tier PvE / A-Tier PvP

Pistols allow you to deal damage while moving — a rare and powerful trait. Their channeled attack lets you kite enemies indefinitely.

Key Abilities:
  • Pistol Volley (Q): Channeled rapid-fire attack that can be aimed while moving. Excellent sustained DPS.
  • Bullet Dance (E): Dodge backward while firing — creates distance without stopping damage.
Pro Tip: Pistols excel against slow melee enemies and bosses. Keep moving, keep firing — most bosses can never touch you.

🌀 Twinblade Oakveil — A-Tier PvE / A-Tier PvP

Introduced in the Invaders of Oakveil expansion, the Twinblade offers rapid slashing attacks with strong freeze synergy. Its high attack speed makes it excellent for applying on-hit effects.

Key Abilities:
  • Whirl Slash (Q): Spinning AoE attack that hits multiple times — excellent freeze application with Frost Amulet.
  • Blink Strike (E): Teleport to target with a follow-up slash. Great mobility and gap closing.
Pro Tip: Twinblade + Frost Amulet is one of the strongest AoE freeze builds in the game. The rapid hits proc freeze faster than any other weapon.

🗡️ Greatsword — B-Tier PvE / A-Tier PvP

The Greatsword hits hard with every swing. Its Great Cleaver ability delivers a devastating 150% weapon damage burst, making it a premier PvP burst weapon.

Key Abilities:
  • Great Cleaver (Q): Charged attack dealing 150% weapon damage in a cone. Oneshots squishy targets in PvP.
  • Rupture (E): Ground slam that slows enemies — sets up the Cleaver combo.
Pro Tip: In PvP, use Rupture to slow, then immediately follow with Great Cleaver. This combo wins fights before they start.

B-TIER — Solid Choices, Outclassed at High Levels

Good weapons that serve specific roles but are generally outclassed by S/A-tier options for serious content.

⚔ Sword

PvE: B / PvP: B

The Sword is your first weapon and the best beginner weapon in V Rising. Its Whirlwind ability provides solid AoE clearing, and the standard moveset is easy to learn. Unfortunately, every role the Sword fills is done better by other weapons at higher gear levels.

  • Whirlwind — Spin attack, solid for early mob camps
  • Shockwave — Ranged slash projectile

🦯 Whip

PvE: B / PvP: B

The Whip's 2-second entangle from range makes it a strong control tool in both PvE and PvP. You can lock down enemies from safe distance. Damage output is lower than other options, but the control makes it viable in coordinated play.

  • Entangle — 2s root at range, excellent setup tool
  • Lash — AoE sweep that pushes enemies back

🏹 Crossbow

PvE: B / PvP: C

The Crossbow offers safe ranged damage with its Rain of Bolts AoE. It's usable in PvE for clearing groups from range, but its slow attack speed and lack of mobility make it inferior to the Longbow in almost every situation.

  • Rain of Bolts — AoE bolt shower, decent clearing
  • Ballista Shot — Heavy single-target damage, slow wind-up

🦅 Claws Oakveil

PvE: C / PvP: B

The Claws are an Oakveil weapon with a Puncture into Rupture combo. They offer decent mobility and bleed damage, but their short range and lower burst potential hold them back compared to established meta picks.

  • Puncture — Quick stab with follow-up Rupture
  • Frenzy — Attack speed buff, enhances the combo

C-TIER — Underwhelming, Niche Use Only

These weapons have fundamental flaws that limit their effectiveness. Only use them if you're specifically building around their niche.

🔨 Mace

PvE: C / PvP: C

The Mace has freeze chain potential — its abilities can chain into freeze effects. The problem: setup time. You need specific amulets and spell builds to make it work. Without the freeze build, the Mace is slow and clunky.

Only if: You're running a dedicated freeze build in a group.

🪓 Axe

PvE: C / PvP: C

The Axe is your first damage weapon and it shows. Its abilities are straight-forward but lackluster. The Reaper outclasses it for AoE; the Spear outclasses it for single-target. The Axe has no unique identity at higher tiers.

Only if: You haven't unlocked better options yet.

🗡️ Throwing Daggers

PvE: C / PvP: D

Throwing Daggers are simply too slow for the damage they deal. Their attack animation has long recovery frames, making them dangerous in PvE and nearly useless in PvP where mobility matters most. The Longbow does everything better.

Only if: You enjoy the challenge or want a break from meta picks.

🔨 Weapon Crafting Tier Progression

V Rising's weapon crafting follows a clear progression path. Each tier requires materials from new biomes and bosses. You craft weapons at the appropriate crafting station, and each tier dramatically increases your gear score and damage output.

Tier Material Gear Score Unlock Method Key Boss
1. Bone Bone + Animal Hide 0-15 Starting equipment (tutorial) —
2. Copper Copper Ingot 15-30 First V Blood weapon unlocks Keely the Frost Archer
3. Iron Iron Ingot 30-50 Unlocked via Iron crafting station Quincey the Bandit King
4. Dark Silver Dark Silver Ingot 50-70 Unlocked by defeating Silverlight bosses Styx the Defiler
5. Sanguine Sanguine Ingot 70-85 Endgame crafting from final zones Adam the First Born
6. Ancestral Ancestral Ingot 85-91 Hallowed Mountains / endgame bosses Dracula
Important: You must unlock each weapon type's recipe separately from V Blood bosses. The crafting tier determines the weapon's gear score and base damage — but the weapon type determines your abilities and playstyle. Always craft your preferred weapon type at the highest available tier.

🎮 Best Weapon by Playstyle

Not everyone plays the same way. Pick the weapon that matches your playstyle to maximize your enjoyment and effectiveness.

🧛 New Player

Best Weapon: Sword

Start with the Sword. Its balanced moveset, easy-to-land Whirlwind AoE, and forgiving attack speed make it the best learning weapon. Switch to Spear or Longbow once you're comfortable with boss mechanics.

Runner-up: Axe (if you prefer slower, harder-hitting attacks)

🎯 Casual Player

Best Weapon: Longbow

If you have limited play time, the Longbow maximizes efficiency. Safe distance means fewer deaths. High DPS means faster boss kills. Focus stacks reward consistent play without complex combos.

Runner-up: Reaper (for satisfying AoE clearing)

🔥 Hardcore PvE Player

Best Weapon: Spear / Reaper

Optimize boss kills with Spear (Harpoon repositioning + A Thousand Spears DPS) and mob clearing with Reaper (Bonestorm + Tendon Swing). Swap between them based on content. Both at Sanguine tier.

Runner-up: Pistols (for no-damage boss fights)

🏆 Completionist / PvP Player

Best Weapons: All 14

Master every weapon type. Use Slasher for PvP stealth kills, Longbow for open-world PvP, Spear for arena combat, Greatsword for burst combos, Twinblade for freeze builds, Whip for control. Each weapon has a place in your arsenal.

Must-learn: Slasher (PvP), Spear (PvP), Longbow (PvE)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best weapon for beginners in V Rising?
The Sword is the best beginner weapon. It's the first weapon you craft, its moveset is easy to learn, and Whirlwind gives you a generous AoE attack for clearing early-game mob camps. Once you reach Copper tier, consider switching to Spear or Longbow for better boss DPS.
Which weapon has the highest DPS in V Rising?
At full Focus stacks, the Longbow has the highest single-target DPS in the game. For burst damage, the Greatsword's Great Cleaver at 150% weapon damage is the single hardest-hitting ability. For sustained AoE DPS, the Reaper is unmatched.
Is the Slasher good for PvE?
The Slasher is mediocre in PvE (B-tier). Its Camouflage ability is useful for sneaking past enemies or setting up a single large backstab, but the low base damage and lack of AoE make it slower than the Reaper, Spear, or Longbow for most PvE content. It's primarily a PvP weapon.
What are the new Oakveil weapons?
The Invaders of Oakveil expansion added three new weapon types: Twinblade (A-tier, fast attacks with freeze synergy), Claws (B-tier PvP, Puncture/Rupture combo), and Throwing Daggers (C-tier, slow ranged damage). Twinblade is the standout — pair it with Frost Amulet for a top-tier freeze build.
Should I craft every weapon type?
Yes, eventually. Each weapon type has unique abilities that are useful in specific situations. At minimum, craft: Reaper (AoE farming), Spear (boss fights), Longbow (safe DPS), and Slasher (PvP). Fill in the rest as you have materials.
Does weapon type affect gear score?
No — gear score is determined by the crafting tier (Bone, Copper, Iron, Dark Silver, Sanguine, Ancestral), not the weapon type. A Sanguine Sword and a Sanguine Reaper have the same gear score. Choose weapon type based on abilities and playstyle.
