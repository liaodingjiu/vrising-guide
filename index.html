❓ Quick Answers

What order should I kill V Blood bosses?
Follow gear score progression: Bone (GS 0-15) → Copper (GS 15-30) → Iron (GS 30-50) → Dark Silver (GS 50-70) → Sanguine (GS 70-91). Each boss unlocks specific crafting, structures, or abilities. Full 64-boss kill order →
What's the best blood type?
Scholar is the meta for PvE (cooldown reset at 60%+ quality). Rogue dominates PvP (armor expose at 90%+). Brute is best for early game survival. Complete blood type guide →
How many weapons are in V Rising?
14 weapon types across 8 gear tiers: Sword, Axe, Mace, Spear, Reaper, Slasher, Greatsword, Longbow, Crossbow, Pistols, Whip, Twinblade (Oakveil), Claws (Oakveil), Throwing Daggers (Oakveil). Full tier list →
Can I move my castle?
Yes. Deconstruct your castle heart, which refunds all materials. All structures in your territory will break down into their component materials. Then craft a new Castle Heart at your desired location. Castle relocation is free — you get 100% materials back.
