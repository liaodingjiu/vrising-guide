Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent, no surprises. Here is exactly what data this site collects and why.

What We Collect

This site uses two analytics services to understand how visitors use it:

  • Microsoft Clarity — Collects anonymized behavioral data: page views, scroll depth, click patterns, and session recordings. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. Clarity does not use cookies for tracking.
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — Collects page view data tied to an anonymized client ID. GA4 sets a first-party cookie to distinguish unique visitors. IP addresses are anonymized by default in GA4.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • No payment or financial data
  • No advertising profiles or tracking across other sites
  • No data is sold to third parties

Your Choices

You can decline analytics cookies via the cookie banner when you first visit. You can also block all analytics by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings or using an ad-blocker. The site works fully without analytics.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect aggregated, anonymized traffic data for DDoS protection and performance monitoring. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.
